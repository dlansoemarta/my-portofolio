Top 3 Romance Anime Movies

1. A Wishker Away

Studio Colorido, Toho Animation, dan Twin Engine

A Whisker Away" tells the story of a girl named Muge who is in her second year of junior high school. In her class, Muge falls in love with a friend named Kento Honode. As a result, Muge constantly tries to get closer to Kento Honode in various ways.

2. Your Name

Masayoshi Tanaka

This anime is about two teenagers named Mitsuha Miyamizu and Taki Tachibana who swap bodies due to a mysterious event. Initially, they are strangers to each other and live in completely different places.

3. The Tunnel to Summer, the Exit of Goodbyes

Tomohisa Taguchi

Natsu e no Tunnel" tells the story of Kaoru Tono, who hears a rumor about the Urashima Tunnel. According to the gossip, anyone who enters the tunnel will have their wishes granted when they reach the end of it. However, there is a price to pay for these wishes. The life of the petitioner will diminish based on the weight of the request they make.
